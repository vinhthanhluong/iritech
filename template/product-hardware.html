IriShield™ Series

IriShield™ Series includes ultra-compact, auto-capture iris scanners, complete with on-board iris recognition and a PKI-based security infrastructure that ensures end-to-end data security.
IriShield™ Series features IriTech’s superior iris matching and iris image quality assessment algorithms to provide good quality images and avoid false positives while maximizing true positive identification rates. The embedded algorithms can complete a matching query against 1,000 stored templates in 0.5 second.
