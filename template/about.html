About IriTech

IriTech, Inc. was established in 2000 in USA with the ultimate goals to develop the most advanced iris recognition technology and products yet ubiquitous to the vast majority of consumers.
Our technology has created various impacts on biometrics market, and is one of very few iris providers who owns all in-house proprietary technologies vital for deploying any scalable end-to-end iris ecosystem.

Customer-centric business
mindset powered by expert team

Beside the headquarter in the U.S.A, IriTech also has offices in South Korea, Viet Nam and India to better serve customers all over the world in a timely and professional manner.

Our Milestone

With more than 20 years of experience in iris recognition technology and top-ranked NIST1)–proven algorithms, IriTech has been trusted by many prestigious clients with the technology being deployed in multiple and multisector large scale projects ranging from governments to corporates due to its exceptional performance in tough environment. Being the first biometric manufacturer in the world receiving STQC certification for both enrollment and authentication device for Indian National ID project (UIDAI), IriTech maintains the leading role in this largest biometric project.
