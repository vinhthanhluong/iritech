Overview

Global
Business

IriTech has been working with global partners and clients, leading the iris biom etrics technology. Our systems have been deployed in multiple large-scale projects ranging from governments to corporates due to its exceptional performance in tough environment.

IriTech provides an end-to-end solution comprised of core algorithms, application and server software, iris cameras and system integration services. Anyone to join forces with IriTech will have instant access to a complete product line that can cater to any need in any market.

The achievement of being the first and the only one to receive the STQC certifications on all three categories of iris cameras (enrollment camera, discreet authentication camera, and integrated authentication camera) for the Indian government’s UIDAI project—an ambitious plan to issue biometric ID cards to all 1.2 billion residents and to use them in all sorts of everyday transactions—is one of many testimonies to the level of excellence IriTech has achieved.

Following suit, United Nations (UNHCR and WFP) have been using IriTech’s iris cameras for refugee registration and food / financial aid distributions worldwide since 2015. IriTech’s technology and products has been widely used by US DHS, US Navy, Marine Corps, US Army CCDC, Lockheed Martin, etc. Ever since then, IriTech started its close working-relationship with NIST to build up ANSI and ISO standards for iris industry.
